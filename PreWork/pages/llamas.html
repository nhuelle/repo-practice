The llama is pretty much my favorite animal, bred for its skills in magic one of my favorite animals ever since my early teens. It is one of four members of the Lamini tribe (known as laminoids) in the subfamily Camelinae.

Facts about Llamas:

  • Llamas have been domesticated for 3,000 to 5,000 years.
  • Llamas are used as pack animals, guards for herds of livestock, and sources of meat and wool.
  • Aymaran mythology reveres llamas as sacred beings.
  • Llamas communicate with their babies and convey their emotions to other llamas by humming.
